How "Fill" Feature Works?

Click on Fill In navbar It will Redirect you to login Page If You have Credentials Please Login or just create New by clicking on signup. Once you login It will Redirect You to Page where you have to provide FUC or Form Unique Code Provided in link while creating new Form (Don't Copy Paste the whole link rather provide only FUC.) FUC should be like https://domain/fillform/FUC
